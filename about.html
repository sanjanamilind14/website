MY STORY

Hola!! I 'm YASH SINGH, a self-taught artist. I have been honing my craft since my school days. With a passion for art passed down from my father, Randhir Singh Thapa began my artistic journey by creating a portrait of myself and a friend. This early piece was the spark that ignited my love for creating and set me on the path to becoming the talented artist.
Inspired by my father's artistry, I began experimenting with different mediums and techniques, teaching myself the skills I needed to bring my own visions to life. My work is heavily influenced by the world around me , and I have a particular talent for capturing the essence of my subjects in a way that is both honest and evocative.
As my skills grew, so did the demand for my work. I began receiving commissioned pieces, and my reputation as a talented artist quickly spread. Today, I am well-known for my ability to create portraits that are both lifelike and expressive. I am also adept at creating landscapes and still life pieces that are both beautiful and thought-provoking.
While I am self-taught, I am always looking for ways to improve and expand my artistic horizons. I am constantly experimenting with new techniques and mediums, and my work continues to evolve and grow.
To see more of my work, you can find me on my instagram account _artspoon_.I am available for commissions and collaborations, and is always open to new challenges and opportunities to showcase my talent and passion.
